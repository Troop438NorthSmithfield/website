About Us

We are a Scouts BSA Troop for girls located in North Smithfield, RI.
Meetings are Friday night, Scouter's Hall in North Smithfield, RI. Call Scoutmaster Josh to confirm our meeting before you stop by!
We have campouts and activities every month.
Join us at the best Scout camp, Yawgoog Scout Reservation, during Week 4 in mid-July.



Troop Goals

  1. Have fun! We want all scouts to enjoy Scouting and take pride in the Troop, patrol, and uniform.
  2. Leadership. We will build a great team of youth leadership to move the Troop forward in the coming years. The Troop will fill several youth leadership positions in the coming months as the Scouts start to learn their way.
  3. Membership. We will grow our Troop to a solid 2-3 patrols so our youth leaders have an opportunity to lead and grow!
  4. Have fun!! We have a great program for this year with summer camp at Camp Yawgoog, many outdoor activities: camping, snow tubing, bicycling and a service project to help our community. All of these will be great opportunities for fun Fun FUN!

Scoutmaster

Hello! I am Scoutmaster Josh Thibeault and I look forward to bringing a great Scouts BSA program to the girls of Troop 438. We're going to have fun and a great Scouting experience. I live in North Smithfield and have 3 children involved in Scouting. I enjoy outdoor activities and learning how to be a better Scout. I was a Boy Scout and made a lifetime of memories. After high school I joined a fire department and the U.S. Army. Both of those choices were influenced by Scouting. I still use the leadership skills I learned in Scouting in my career as Foreman and Senior Technician at Anchor Subaru, the largest service department in New England.
